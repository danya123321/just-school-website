Babur's birthday

Babur ( 14 February 1483 – 26 December 1530), born Zahīr ud-Dīn Muhammad, was the founder of the Mughal Empire and first Emperor of the Mughal dynasty (r. 1526–1530) in the Indian subcontinent. He was a descendant of Timur and Genghis Khan through his father and mother respectively. He was also given the posthumous name of Firdaws Makani. February 14 really entered the history of Uzbekistan. And of course, our school also celebrated this meaningful day. (more photos in our facebook)
